# Quick Start Guide

Follow these simple steps to get your website up and running in 30 minutes!

## Step 1: Prepare Your Materials (10 minutes)

Gather these items:
- [ ] A good quality profile photo (square, at least 500x500px)
- [ ] Your resume or CV (for reference)
- [ ] Recent payslips (last 3 months)
- [ ] Educational certificates
- [ ] Professional certifications
- [ ] Employment letter
- [ ] Form 16 (tax certificate)

## Step 2: Add Your Photo (2 minutes)

1. Rename your profile photo to `profile.jpg`
2. Copy it to `assets/images/` folder
3. Make sure it's a clear, professional photo

## Step 3: Fill in Your Details (15 minutes)

Open `index.html` in a text editor (VS Code, Notepad++, or even Notepad) and update:

### Must Update:
1. **Line 31**: Your name in `<h1>` tag
2. **Lines 55-90**: All personal details in About section
3. **Lines 105-130**: Your work experience
4. **Lines 145-175**: Your skills
5. **Lines 200-225**: Your certifications and awards
6. **Lines 240-265**: Your package details
7. **Lines 290-310**: Your contact information

### Pro Tip:
Use Find & Replace (Ctrl+H) to quickly replace:
- "Your Name" with your actual name
- "your.email@example.com" with your email
- "+91 XXXXX XXXXX" with your phone number

## Step 4: Add Your Documents (5 minutes)

1. Go to `assets/documents/` folder
2. Copy your PDF documents there
3. Rename them to match the names in the website:
   - `payslip.pdf`
   - `employment-letter.pdf`
   - `form16.pdf`
   - `id-proof.pdf`
   - `education.pdf`
   - `certifications.pdf`

**IMPORTANT**: Before adding, redact sensitive info like:
- Bank account numbers
- Complete PAN/Aadhaar numbers (show only last 4 digits)
- Full signatures

## Step 5: Test Your Website (3 minutes)

1. Double-click `index.html` to open in your browser
2. Check all sections load correctly
3. Verify your photo appears
4. Test if documents download
5. Try the contact form

## Step 6: Deploy to GitHub Pages (10 minutes)

### First Time Setup:

1. Create GitHub account at https://github.com (if you don't have one)
2. Create new repository named `your-username.github.io`
3. Download GitHub Desktop from https://desktop.github.com

### Upload Your Website:

1. Open GitHub Desktop
2. Click "Add" > "Add Existing Repository"
3. Select the `marriage-profile-website` folder
4. Click "Publish repository"
5. Uncheck "Keep this code private" (or keep private if you prefer)
6. Click "Publish repository"

### Make it Live:

1. Go to your repository on GitHub.com
2. Click "Settings"
3. Scroll to "Pages" section
4. Under "Source", select "main" branch
5. Click "Save"
6. Wait 2-3 minutes
7. Your website is live at `https://your-username.github.io`

## Step 7: Share Your Website

Once live, share your website link with:

- Relatives

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Forgetting to update placeholder text** - Search for "Your Name", "XXXXX", etc.
2. **Not testing locally first** - Always open index.html in browser before deploying
3. **Uploading original documents without redaction** - Always redact sensitive info
4. **Using very large image files** - Compress your profile photo to under 1MB
5. **Not updating all contact info** - Double-check email, phone, LinkedIn
